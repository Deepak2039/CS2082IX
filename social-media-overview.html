5.2 Social Media Overview

What is Social Media?

Social media refers to websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking. It has become an integral part of modern communication and digital culture.

Key Characteristics

  • User-generated content
  • Interactive platforms
  • Real-time communication
  • Network-based interactions
  • Community building

Types of Social Media

Social Networks

Platforms that allow users to connect with friends, family, and colleagues.

Examples: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter

Media Sharing

Platforms focused on sharing photos, videos, and other media.

Examples: Instagram, YouTube, TikTok

Discussion Forums

Platforms for in-depth discussions on various topics.

Examples: Reddit, Quora, Stack Exchange

Blogging & Publishing

Platforms for publishing articles and long-form content.

Examples: Medium, WordPress, Blogger

Impact of Social Media

Positive Impacts

  • Global connectivity
  • Business opportunities
  • Educational resources
  • Social movements
  • Information sharing

Challenges

  • Privacy concerns
  • Cyberbullying
  • Misinformation
  • Addiction
  • Mental health impact

Social Media Etiquette

Practicing good digital manners helps create a positive online environment for everyone.

Do's

  • Think before you post
  • Respect others' opinions
  • Give credit for shared content
  • Keep personal information private
  • Report inappropriate content

Don'ts

  • Share false information
  • Engage in cyberbullying
  • Overshare personal details
  • Post inappropriate content
  • Ignore privacy settings
